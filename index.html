About Fortnite

Fortnite is one of the most popular battle royale games in the world, developed by Epic Games.

Fortnite Game Modes

  • Battle Royale: A classic mode where players drop into a map and fight for survival.
  • Creative: A sandbox mode where players can create their own worlds and experiences.
  • Save the World: A cooperative game mode where players fight off waves of enemies called husks.

Why Fortnite is Popular

Fortnite has gained popularity because of its unique combination of building mechanics,gameplay, and constant updates with new seasons, items, and skins.
